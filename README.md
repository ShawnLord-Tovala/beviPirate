# beviPirate

A mobile web app to control Bevi water machines by scanning their touchless QR codes.

## Scan to open

![QR code](qr.png)

**https://shawnlord-tovala.github.io/beviPirate/**

## How it works

1. Open the app on your phone
2. Scan the QR code on a Bevi machine
3. The control panel opens automatically
4. Pick your flavors, set intensity, choose water type, and hold the button to pour
